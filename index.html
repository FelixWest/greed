Game Rules

In "Greed," players roll five dice (represented by the letters G, R, E, E, D) to achieve the highest score.


  • 5 of a kind: 2000 points
  • 4 of a kind: 2 times the letter value
  • 3 of a kind: 1 time the letter value
  • Each "E" rolled: 100 points

The first player to reach 5000 points wins the game!


Important: You must click Hold after rolling to save your points. If you don't and continue rolling, you risk rolling a farkle (a non-scoring combination) and losing all points from the current round.
